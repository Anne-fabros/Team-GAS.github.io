What is Streaming?

Streaming refers to a multimedia file such as audio and video that can be instantly played without the need to download it first. A media content, recorded or live, can be considered a streamed content as long as it can be played on any device via the internet even if you do not download it. It works through a continuous transmission of the media content from a server to the client. The following are the most common forms of streamed content:

  • Video Game

What is Live Streaming?

Live Streaming is a broadcast that allows you to watch or listen to media content in real time. It is different in those pre-recorded streamed contents in terms of your capacity to control how you want to watch it; you can easily pause the pre-recorded content and still go back to where you left it. However, in livestreams, you can pause it but you cannot go back to where you left it, as it happens real time. The following are some examples of public and private live streaming applications:

  • Facebook Live
  • Twitch TV
  • Youtube Live
  • Skype
  • Zoom
